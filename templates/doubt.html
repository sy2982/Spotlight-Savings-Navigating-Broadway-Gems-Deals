Doubt: A Parable

"Doubt: A Parable" is a gripping drama written by John Patrick Shanley. Set in a Catholic school in the Bronx in 1964, the play explores themes of uncertainty, faith, and moral ambiguity. The story follows Sister Aloysius, the strict and conservative principal, who becomes suspicious of Father Flynn's relationship with one of the students, leading to a tense confrontation.
