Heathers: High School, Hierarchy, and Homicide

"Heathers" is a dark comedy rock musical based on the 1989 cult film of the same name. With music, lyrics, and a book by Laurence O'Keefe and Kevin Murphy, it satirizes the complexities of high school life and teenage angst, set against the backdrop of 1980s America.

What You'll Discover:
- Story: The tumultuous and treacherous world of Westerberg High, where Veronica Sawyer navigates the social hierarchy and the notorious Heathers.
- Music and Lyrics: Engaging songs that blend dark humor with electrifying rock influences.
- Impact: Explore how "Heathers" challenges social norms and portrays the darker side of teenage life.
