The Wiz

"The Wiz" is a beloved Broadway musical reimagining of "The Wizard of Oz," with a soulful and vibrant twist. Featuring an all-black cast and a fusion of Motown, gospel, and R&B music, the show follows Dorothy as she journeys through the magical land of Oz, encountering memorable characters like the Scarecrow, Tin Man, and Cowardly Lion along the way.
