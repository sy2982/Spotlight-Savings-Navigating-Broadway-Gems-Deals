My Fair Lady

"My Fair Lady" is a classic Broadway musical based on George Bernard Shaw's play "Pygmalion." Set in Edwardian London, the story follows Eliza Doolittle, a Cockney flower girl, who undergoes a transformation under the guidance of phonetics professor Henry Higgins. With its memorable songs and timeless story of self-discovery and love, "My Fair Lady" has enchanted audiences for generations.

What You'll Discover:
- Story: Experience the charming tale of Eliza's journey from rags to refinement in Edwardian London.
- Characters: Meet the memorable characters of Professor Higgins, Colonel Pickering, and Eliza Doolittle.
- Music: Enjoy the unforgettable songs including "I Could Have Danced All Night" and "The Rain in Spain."
