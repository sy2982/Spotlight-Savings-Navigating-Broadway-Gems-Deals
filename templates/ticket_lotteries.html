Ticket Lotteries

- Your Chance for the Spotlight

  1. Review the list of shows offering digital lotteries. Find ones you’re interested in and go to the lottery websites.
  2. Choose a show and click 'Enter Lottery' to begin the process. Submit your entry.
  3. Lottery entries close 24-48 hours before the show. Keep an eye on your email for the results.
  4. Winners are notified a few hours before the show. Check if you've won and proceed to purchase.
  5. If you've won, follow the link in your winner’s email to secure and pay for your tickets.
