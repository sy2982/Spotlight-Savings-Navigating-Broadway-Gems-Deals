Wicked: The Untold Story of the Witches of Oz

"Wicked" takes a fresh look at the Land of Oz, telling the story from the perspective of the witches. With music and lyrics by Stephen Schwartz and book by Winnie Holzman, this musical offers a parallel narrative to "The Wizard of Oz." The show explores themes of friendship, justice, and the nature of evil.

What You'll Discover:
- Story: The background story of Elphaba, the misunderstood, so-called Wicked Witch of the West.
- Music and Lyrics: Experience the captivating score including hits like "Defying Gravity."
- Impact: Learn how "Wicked" has captivated audiences around the world and its impact on modern musical theatre.
