Hadestown: The Myth. The Musical.

"Hadestown" intertwines two mythic tales — that of young dreamers Orpheus and Eurydice, and that of King Hades and his wife Persephone — as it invites you on a hell-raising journey to the underworld and back. This innovative musical is a haunting and hopeful theatrical experience that grabs you and never lets go.

What You'll Discover:
- Story: Explore the reimagined Greek myths woven into a compelling narrative.
- Music and Lyrics: Experience the vibrant and soulful music by Anaïs Mitchell.
- Impact: Understand the unique staging and direction that have made "Hadestown" a modern classic.
